So what's this all about?

If you would like to take the same, highly reviewed courses that I offer on Udemy, but you would prefer some additional hand holding, then this is your solution. And this personalized solution comes without the high cost that is usually associated with class-room training.

Both the Platinum and Gold levels are set up to provide personalized feedback as you move through the training. Each time you finish an exercise, you submit it and I will get back to you with feedback within 2 business days. You can send questions to my personal email at any time throughout the training. And if you need more structure, we can create a schedule that will insure you finish the course by a given date.

If you chooose the platinum level, you also get to participate in a personal web conference were I address specific questions or topics you are struggling to master.

Platinum Training

At the platinum level, you will gain full access to the entire course and all of the exercise files. And in addition you will receive the following benefits:

  • Personalized feedback on all exercises.
  • Unlimited questions to the trainer.
  • Personalized schedule for completing the course.
  • Two 30 minute live sessions with the trainer.

Once you sign up for the course using the platinum level, in addition to receiving access to the course, you will receive a welcome email that provides you with personalized access to the trainier. The first email you will send is informaion on how fast you would like to complete the course. This will allow the trainer to establish your personalized schedule. This is the same email you will use to submit completed exercises and any questions you have. You will also be able to plan your live session with the trainer.
